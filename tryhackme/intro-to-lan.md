## 🎓 Learning Objectives
According to TryHackMe, in this room you will learn:
- **LAN Topologies:** Exploring design patterns for private networks (Star, Bus, Ring).
- **Subnetting Primer:** Basics of how networks are divided.
- **ARP (Address Resolution Protocol):** How devices map IP addresses to physical MAC addresses.
- **DHCP (Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol):** How IP addresses are automatically assigned.

## 🧠 Knowledge Required
- **Foundational:** Recommended to have a basic understanding of what a network is.
