## 🎓 Learning Objectives
According to TryHackMe, in this room you will learn:
- **Port Forwarding:** Allowing external access to internal services.
- **Firewalls:** Principles of network traffic filtering.
- **VPN Basics:** Securely extending private networks over public ones.
- **LAN Networking Devices:** Understanding routers, switches, and other hardware.

## 🧠 Knowledge Required
- **Networking Basics:** Familiarity with the OSI model and IP addressing.
