## 🎓 Learning Objectives
According to TryHackMe, in this room you will learn:
- **HTTP/S Protocol:** Understanding requests, responses, and secure communication.
- **Methods & Status Codes:** Differentiating GET, POST, PUT, DELETE, and various responses (200, 404, 500 etc.).
- **Headers & Cookies:** How metadata and state are managed in web traffic.
- **Practical Interaction:** Making and inspecting web requests.

## 🧠 Knowledge Required
- **Foundational:** Basic understanding of how websites are accessed.
