## 🎓 Learning Objectives
According to TryHackMe, in this room you will learn:
- **Offensive Security:** Basics of the hacker mindset and legal hacking.
- **Finding Weaknesses:** How to identify vulnerabilities in web applications.
- **Exploiting Weaknesses:** Practical exercise in hacking a web app to understand how to improve its security.

## 🧠 Knowledge Required
- **Beginner Level:** No prior experience in offensive security is required.
